The d-optimal designs for gradient and isocratic conditions. Explicit solutions.

Gradient conditions - LSS equation. Approximat solution for highly retained analytes (logkw>>0)

% gradient conditions
syms tg1 positive
syms tg2 positive
syms dfi positive
syms S positive
syms to positive
syms logkw positive
% td = 0, te = 0, fio = 0
% 1 is neglected (logkw is high)
tr1 = to+ tg1/dfi*S*log(log(10)*to*dfi*S*10^(logkw)/tg1);
tr2 = to+ tg2/dfi*S*log(log(10)*to*dfi*S*10^(logkw)/tg2);
 
g1 = gradient(tr1,[logkw,S]);
g2 = gradient(tr2,[logkw,S]);
 
g = [g1 g2];
detFIMgra = simplify(det(g'*g));
